I'm a designer and a storyteller. But mostly, I'm a problem solver. Currently, I work for Vox Media. I've also done print design for the Indianapolis Star and graphics reporting for the Chicago Tribune.

Much of my work isn't tangible. I've spent a lot of my career figuring out ways to help other people get what they need to reach their goals. The best team is an empowered one.

As editor-in-chief of The Ball State Daily News, I motivated a volunteer staff and worked with them to identify and solve problems in our workflow. Though only a student paper, the skills I learned through The Daily News translate well to professional work environments.

For instance, at Vox Media, I've spent a lot of team working with the staff of Vox.com, helping figure out alternative ways to tell stories and identifying best practices for news tools.

More importantly, I've tried to listen to the needs of the staff to determine what new tools can help them better share their reporting.
