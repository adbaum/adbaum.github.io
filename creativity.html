Playing with ad styles

Liquor store

I made this piece after seeing some really incredible work from Vox Media. They have remarkable and unconventional ad spots that I think are far more enticing than standard positions.

I wanted to get BallStateDaily.com up to par.

The ad at once commands attention and falls away. It upstages the content without being flashy. Instead of using awkward and forced animation to grab attention, it latches onto a natural user instinct: the scroll.

It almost gives the user control over the ad.

I also see amazing editorial potential for something like this.

Though I don't know exactly what story might lend itself to this sort of tool, I am actively seeking the opportunity to implement it.
