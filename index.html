The Daily News

The Daily News publishes a print product four days a week, as well as online to ballstatedaily.com everyday. I am currently editor-in-chief, though I have also served as a designer, reporter, graphics editor and social media coordinator.

Under my leadership, the staff has gained a strong focus on breaking news, covering stories such as a public figure at Ball State tweeting racially insensitive remarks, the university president resigning and reports of a possible gunman on campus.

Our primary objectives for the upcoming months include an emphasis on enterprise reporting and a survey of the student body to determine what people want from their student paper.

Unified Media

The Daily News and other student media organizations have recently begun to collaborate and generate under the name Unified Media. This content then flows to multiple platforms, including the newspaper, radio broadcast and website.

As a the editor of The Daily News, it is my responsibility to encourage and be open to cooperative efforts with the student radio and television broadcasts, as well as the student magazine.

Furthermore, I helped lead the redesign of ballstatedaily.com to emphasize more multimedia elements.

In the near future, Unified Media will see the consilidation of its design and possibly copy resources, increasing efficiency and offering a wider range of experience to student participants.
