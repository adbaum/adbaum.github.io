Site Redesigns

I've got a couple under my belt.

Racked.com is one of the sites Vox Media acquired in 2013. In 2015, it relaunched with new branding, new design, and a new editorial vision.

I worked on much of the initial design exploration, putting together a competitive analysis, font boards, and mood boards. I also worked on some of the comps prior to development.

These are some of my mood boards. Each has a name to describe the tones – part of a strategy intended to make design languages clearer to stakeholders.

I worked under the direction of people such as Georgia Cowley and Jason Santa Maria, who taught me how to research and develop design languages, as well as how to design for systems.

Daily Xtra

I also acted as a designer for an LGBTQ news site in Canada called Daily Xtra. The organization has newsrooms in Toronto, Ottawa and Canada. They had previously had a biweekly print product before switching to a digital model in 2014.

I worked with a user experience consultant's wireframes to create the design language of the site, which will spread to future iterations, including a move to a responsive design.
